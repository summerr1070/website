Games You Can Play Online

On this page you will find games that your child can play online. Each game has a brief explanation, a link to the website the game is on, and what type of math the game focuses on. Games that have “any” for the math skill means that it can work for addition, subtraction, multiplication, or division.



Info:

Math bingo is a fun and educational way to play bingo. Insead of the numbers being called out like usual the child is given a math problem and mark of the answer. As they gain points they are also able to unlock and collect “bingo bugs” which provide nice incentive to keep practicing.

Info:

This game is a racing game where you are racing either other people or computers to get to the finish line first. You are given multiple choice division questions and when you answer one right you are accelerated forward. This game allows for kids to play and compete against their friends, siblings, etc.

Math skill:

Division

Link:

Drag Race Division

Info:

This is a timed game where you try to match 2 yarn balls that have numbers on them to make a number that is in the middle. For example if the number in the middle is 9 you have to try and find 2 numbers that make 9 out of the yarn balls on the table. Each pair you make gets you 1 point and the most points wins. This game can be played with computers or other people.
